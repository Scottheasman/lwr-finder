📋 2025 Florida Property Tax Rates — Sarasota & Manatee Counties

Sarasota County (Waterside, Skye Ranch, Palmer Ranch): Total millage ~11.47 mills = effective rate ~1.05–1.15% of assessed value. New construction assessed at purchase price (no homestead cap year 1). With $50K homestead exemption: saves ~$575/yr on county + school taxes.

Manatee County (LWR Main, most villages): Total millage ~14.5–16 mills depending on district = effective rate ~1.25–1.45% of assessed value. Higher than Sarasota due to school district millage.

⚠️ New construction buyers: year 1 taxes are calculated on full purchase price. Apply for Homestead Exemption by March 1 following your move-in to save ~$550–$900/yr from year 2 onward. CDD fees appear separately on your tax bill and are NOT included in the above rates.

⚠️ 2025 PMI Rates — Required When Down Payment < 20%

PMI protects the lender (not you) if you default. Required on conventional loans with <20% down. Drops off automatically at 22% equity (or request at 20%).
